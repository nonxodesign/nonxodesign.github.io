    • We were invited to create a series of visual design films for Chi Po-lin Foundation X Zero Waste Fashion - Story Wear.
    The denim color of recycled fabrics combined with the concept of fashion designers, the stardust universe shimmering from the
    convergence of bits and pieces, to develop sustainability Pioneer of fashion.

    -
    我們受邀為看見齊柏林基金會 × 零廢棄時尚 Story Wear 進行動畫與實體拍攝合成設計。
    以回收布料的丹寧色系結合服裝設計師的理念，點滴匯流而成的星塵宇宙微光，開拓永續時尚的先驅。

    Under
    the infinite universe
    and ocean

    在 無 盡 的 宇 宙 和 海 洋 之 下


    With the skillful hands of Story Wear's designers, discarded fabrics are recycled into
    meaningful fashion pieces, and we are deeply influenced by this concept to create the blue
    and purple stardust, which symbolizes each person, each garment, and each story, weaving our
    precious ocean and universe again in an infinite cycle.

    在 Story Wear 服裝設計師的巧手下，廢棄的布料回收再製成了一件件富有意義的時裝，
    被深深影響的我們延伸了這樣的概念創作了藍紫色的星塵，遍布的星光象徵每一個人、每一件衣服、每一個故事，
    無限地循環再次編織了我們珍貴的海洋和宇宙。


    The perpetual fashion allows us to confidently step on the mirrored stage reflecting the infinite starlight and
    wander under the endless expanse.

    永續的時尚將使我們能夠自信地踏在反射無限星光的鏡面舞台，
    徜徉在無盡的遼闊之下。
